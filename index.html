About Us

Born from the earth.
Built for global trade.

Serendib Lanka (PVT) LTD is a Colombo-based exporter with deep roots in Sri Lanka's agricultural heartland. We source directly from growers and certified processing facilities, eliminating intermediaries so you receive authentic products at competitive prices.

Our team understands both the island's centuries-old cultivation traditions and the rigorous compliance demands of European, American, and Asian markets. Every shipment leaves with complete documentation — COA, phytosanitary certificates, organic certifications, and full traceability.

FAQ

Common questions.

What is your minimum order quantity?

We accommodate samples (1–5 kg), trial orders (50–200 kg), and full container loads. MOQ varies by product — contact us and we'll be transparent about minimums for your specific requirements.

Which certifications can you supply documentation for?

EU Organic (EU 2018/848), USDA NOP, JAS, BRCGS, ISO 22000, Halal (SLBFE/MJA), Fairtrade, Rainforest Alliance, and Global G.A.P. — depending on the supplier and product.

Do you ship to the EU, US, and UK?

Yes. We have established freight relationships with forwarders experienced in EU, UK, US, Gulf, Australian, and Japanese import compliance. We can quote DDP, CIF, or FOB as needed.

How long does the sampling process take?

Samples typically reach European addresses within 7–10 working days via DHL Express. COA and certifications are sent digitally in advance of physical arrival.

Can you create private-label or custom-formulated products?

Absolutely. Several of our production partners offer private-label packaging, custom blends, and OEM manufacturing. Lead times and MOQs vary — let's discuss your brief.

Are you a trading company or a manufacturer?

We are a sourcing consultancy and export agency. We do not manufacture ourselves — instead we maintain a curated, audited network of certified Sri Lankan producers and act as your permanent representative in-country.
